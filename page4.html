Billie Eilish Pirate Baird O'Connel is a singer, songwriter. She collaborates with her brother, Finneas Pirate Baird O'connel, her producer and co-writer. And from an early age of 13 she had been writing and producing songs with her brother from their home in their bedroom. Which songs like "Ocean Eyes", "bad guy" and an album called "WHEN WE ALL FALL ASLEEP, WHERE DO WE GO?" were made. Now, they are one of the most famous songwriters of the decade.

HOW IT ALL STARTED

From an early age of 13 she had recorded "Ocean Eyes", written by her brother. This song became famous across the states and beyond. It gained recognition in 2015. From there it started it all.

Her EP "Dont smile at me" charted at #14 at the Billboard 200 Chart. Which was written by Billie and Finneas and produced by Finneas. At a young age of 15 she was off to a great start.

THE RISING STAR

Soon after she released the famous album called "WHEN WE ALL FALL ASLEEP, WHERE DO WE GO", in March 29, 2019. It charted at #1 in the Billboard 200 Chart. Which "Bad Guy" was its number one song. And swept many awards for the 2020 Grammys. Such as "Song of the year" "Record of the year". Also, won "Album of the Year" "Best Pop Vocal Album of the Year" and "Best New Artist". From here she kept releasing hit after hit. And she did not stop

Songs like "Everything I wanted" "No time to die" "What was I Made For?" had hit the charts. From the year 2021-2024 these songs swept many awards. Such as "Song of the Year" "Best Original Song" and many more. On top of that she was proclaimed as the youngest person to ever win 2 Oscars at only the age of 22.

Currently she has 9 Grammys, 2 Oscars, 2 American Music Awards, 2 MTV Europe Music Awards, 3 MTV Video Music Awards, 2 Academy Awards, 2 Guinness World Records, 1 Brit Award, 3 Billboard Music Awards. Which she had won from the ages of 15-22.
